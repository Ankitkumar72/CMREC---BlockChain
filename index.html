CMREC BlockChain Club

The CMREC Blockchain Club is a student-led organization dedicated to bringing high-quality presentations and workshops to the students of CMREC.

The club aims to equip people with the foundational knowledge to reason Blockchain developments from an engineering perspective, taking into account technical, economic and policy factors.

We want to build a community where students and faculty can come together to network and learn from each other, as well as collaborate on new and existing ideas.
